Spaghetti Recipe

Classic Spaghetti Bolognese is a timeless Italian dish featuring al dente spaghetti topped
with a rich meat sauce of ground beef, onions, and tomatoes, seasoned to perfection.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Paste
  • Crushed Tomato
  • Dried Oregano
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese

Steps:

  1. In a pan, saut√© onions and garlic. Add ground beef, cook until browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Boil spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain.
  4. Toss cooked pasta with the sauce. Serve with grated Parmesan.
  5. Enjoy your classic spaghetti Bolognese!
