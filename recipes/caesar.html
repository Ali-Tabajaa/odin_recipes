Caesar Salad Recipe

Caesar Salad is a classic favorite, showcasing crisp romaine lettuce tossed in a
creamy Caesar dressing. Topped with crunchy croutons and grated Parmesan
cheese, this salad offers a perfect blend of textures and flavors for a
refreshing and satisfying dish.

Ingredients:

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Caesar Dressing
  • Lemon Wedges
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese

Steps:

  1. Combine lettuce, croutons, and Parmesan in a bowl.
  2. Add Caesar dressing and toss until evenly coated.
  3. Serve on plates, garnish with lemon wedges.
  4. Enjoy your refreshing Caesar salad!
